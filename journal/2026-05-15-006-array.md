## What I learned

`ignore` upstream doesn't automatically mean "render source-only". The reason matters: `primitives.md` uses `ignore` for a *deliberate compile error* the chapter teaches; this chapter uses `ignore` defensively for *commented-out panic lines*. The first stays source-only; the second can be evaluated normally.
