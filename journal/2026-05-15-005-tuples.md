## What happened

Watch was already running with a warm cache (9 prior snippets cache-hit on startup). Saved `primitives/tuples.typ`, added the `#include`, and the watcher picked up the change immediately — eval cycle ran in well under the cold-compile 30s window (the type machinery is shared across snippets after the earlier evcxr `:cache 500` warm-up). `rbe-primitives-tuples.txt` materialized with the upstream output verbatim:

## What I learned

The watch loop is the right authoring path. Edit → ~1–2s feedback cycle for snippets that share type with prior compiles. The deferred-eval pattern I used for the earlier today's chapter PRs was unnecessary friction — verification belongs in the loop, not in a follow-up.
